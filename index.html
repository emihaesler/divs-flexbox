Hi, my name is Maria Gonzalez Haesler.

I am a responsive designer and developer who thrives on visual storytelling. When I'm not working, pursuing side projects or getting lost in the rabbit hole of blogs & books, I can be found traveling, hiking, and searching for new stories.

I was raised in South Florida, but I was born in Argentina, so I owe a lot of my mannerisms and characteristics to being exposed to a blend of Argentine and American culture. Thankfully, this includes my bilingualism, since I am fluent in Spanish and English!

One of my favorite pasttimes outside of coding and web dev is spending time being active outside in nature, or just moving my body indoors, like at the gym! I think it helps create a wonderful balance and break from the otherwise sedentary life we tend to lead as coders!

A fun fact about me: I chose a vegan diet in 2017 and never looked back! I always get asked if I miss cheese or any other non-vegan foods. The answer to that is that the only thing I miss is fish and (real) sushi, but vegan sushi and other vegan dishes are just as healthy and delicious so it's not a huge loss.
